# Glossary

## A
**ADR (Architecture Decision Record)**  
A short document capturing a significant architectural decision and its rationale.

## B
**Block**  
A building block in the Residential Society layout containing multiple units.

## C
**Contribution**  
A payment for monthly services and celebration of yearly events by an individual.

**Contribution Heads**
Monthly services (maintenance, Gym, Swimming Pool) of celebration of yearly events such as festivals and feasts.

**Contribution Rates**
An amount against contribution heads that may be revised from time to time.

**Contribution Periods**
An year or its months that get associated with contributions

**Contribution Details**
A record that associates a payment with a contribution period.

## I
**Individual**  
A person associated with a unit as an owner or resident or simply makes a contribution (payment).

## U
**Unit**  
A residential space, also called a flat within a block.
